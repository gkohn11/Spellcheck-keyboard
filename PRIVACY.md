# Privacy Policy for Simple Spellcheck

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Data Collection

**Simple Spellcheck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

We don't store your data. Period.

## What We Don't Do

- We do not collect any typing data, keystrokes, or text you type
- We do not transmit any data to external servers
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not collect device information or user identifiers
- We do not share data with third parties

## Local Data Storage

All text replacement data and settings are stored locally on your device only:
- Text replacement entries (CSV files) are stored in the app's private internal storage
- Keyboard settings and preferences are stored in Android's standard SharedPreferences
- No data leaves your device unless you send the csv file.

## Permissions

Simple Spellcheck requests minimal permissions:
- **Vibrate**: Used for haptic feedback when typing (optional, can be disabled)
- **Storage (Android 9 and below)**: Only used for importing/exporting CSV files for text replacements
